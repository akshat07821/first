Dhurandar

Jaskirat Singh Rangi (Ranveer Singh) loses his family to political violence and takes revenge. He is recruited by Indian intelligence and assumes the alias Hamza Ali Mazari. As Hamza, he infiltrates Karachi’s underworld, kills gangster Rehman Dakait, and manipulates rival factions. He rises to power as “Sher-e-Baloch”, becoming both a feared gangster and a political force. The film blends revenge, espionage, and geopolitics, referencing real events like Operation Lyari and Pakistan’s political turmoil.
